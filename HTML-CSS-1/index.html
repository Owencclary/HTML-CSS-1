The Hunger Games: The Ballard of Songbirds and Snakes is a prequel to the original Hunger Games trilogy. At the prize ceromany the rules change, the prize is no longer given to the student with the best grades but the student who can mentor a tribute to win the Hunger Games.

Mr. Snow receives Mary Lou as his tribute to mentor, Mary Loy is a mentally unstable girl who likes to sing. Mary Lou being weak, Mr. Snow knows his only chance of getting her to win is from sponsors, so he tries to make her singing popular with the audience.
